Privacy policy

Burn Rate Coach is an independent Chrome extension that adds pacing guidance to the Codex Analytics usage page. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by OpenAI.

Information handled

Burn Rate Coach processes quota information already visible on the Codex Analytics usage page: remaining percentages, meter names, reset times, and values derived from those fields. It also stores the settings you choose, including whether the coaching interface is shown, your target remaining percentage, tolerance, and history preference.

When local history is enabled, the extension stores timestamped quota percentages and reset timestamps in Chrome’s local extension storage. Each time the Analytics page processes history, records older than 10 days are ignored and pruned and each meter is capped at 2,600 samples. If the Analytics page is not opened again, older stored records can remain on the device until you clear history or uninstall the extension.

Burn Rate Coach does not read or store prompts, conversations, source code, passwords, authentication cookies, payment information, or API keys.

How information is used

The information is used only to calculate and display current pacing position, recommended action, local trajectories, projected quota remaining at reset, and the latest active/unrecognized compatibility state.

Storage and transmission

Settings, compatibility state, and quota history remain in chrome.storage.local on your device. Burn Rate Coach does not operate a backend, transmit quota information to the developer or third parties, use analytics or advertising SDKs, or sell user data.

If you choose an external support or privacy link, Chrome opens that public website. The website processes the visit under its own privacy policy. Burn Rate Coach does not append tracking parameters.

Sharing

Burn Rate Coach does not share or transfer extension data to third parties. It does not use data for advertising, profiling, creditworthiness, or purposes unrelated to quota pacing.

Your controls

Turning off Show coach removes Burn Rate Coach guidance from the page but does not stop page observation or compatibility checks. If Local pace history is enabled, local sampling continues while the Analytics page is open so guidance can resume with current evidence when shown again. Turning off Local pace history stops collecting and using saved samples whether the coach is shown or hidden. Existing samples remain on the device until the next Analytics-page cleanup, an explicit clear, or uninstall, and can inform projections again if you re-enable history first. The two-click Clear history control remains available while the coach is hidden, deletes current and legacy quota snapshots, and prevents an in-flight older write from restoring them. If Local pace history remains enabled, collection can begin again with later page observations. Uninstalling Burn Rate Coach removes its local extension data through Chrome.

Security and policy compliance

All executable code is packaged with the extension. Burn Rate Coach uses information only for its disclosed single purpose and follows the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

Changes

Material changes to data handling will be reflected in this policy, the Chrome Web Store privacy disclosures, and—when required—an in-product disclosure before the changed handling begins.
