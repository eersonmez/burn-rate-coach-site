Private by design

Your quota stays in Chrome.

Burn Rate Coach reads only visible meter labels, remaining percentages, and reset times on the Codex Analytics usage page. Settings and optional snapshots remain in chrome.storage.local.

There is no backend, analytics SDK, advertising, remote code, cookie access, or account system.

Know the limits

A coach, not a guarantee.

  • History is observed only while the Analytics page is open.
  • Percentage points are not tasks, tokens, messages, or minutes.
  • Projections can change after bursts of heavier or lighter work.
  • The 1.0 parser and interface are English-language only.
